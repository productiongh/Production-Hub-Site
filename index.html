About Us

Production Hub GH is a leading multimedia and live streaming company based in Accra, Ghana. We specialize in delivering high-quality video production services for events such as conferences, church programs, musical concerts, funerals, weddings, and more.

With a skilled crew, cutting-edge equipment, and a passion for excellence, we go beyond capturing moments — we create powerful visual stories that connect audiences everywhere.

Whether it’s a multi-camera live stream, drone footage, or cinematic event coverage, our goal is simple: To make your audience feel present — no matter where they are.
